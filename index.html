What Are Salmon Cookies?

You might be wondering what a salmon cookie even is, and we are thrilled to tell you that they are exactly what it sounds like! We introduce to you, our world-famous cookies created with love from a secret family recipe! These cookies are guaranteed to have you coming back for more so that you can share them with your friends and family! Our first ever location opened it's doors in Seattle, Wa in 2017 and the love for these unique treats has since been discovered and shared around the world! Currently spreading joy out of 5 location, you can find our cookies in Tokyo, Lima, Paris, Dubai - and of course in the great PNW! Give us a call, put in an order ahead of time to beat the lines, or even better - just come on by to try them fresh out of the oven!

Meet Our Family

We are the Cutters! Karen Cutter and husband Tom Cutter met on a deep sea fishing tour in 2007 and our love for salmon brought us together! Tom has always had a love for baking and Karen dreamt of one day owning a small local business in her hometown, Seattle, Wa. Together they have two wonderful children who have grown to love baking just as much as Tom. You may be wondering where the idea of salmon cookies came from. When our youngest, Jonny was 4 years old we found he had gotten into the bag of salmon flavored dog treat. It was easy to see just how much he enjoyed the delicious flavor so we decided to create a new human friendly recipe. After sharing with some friends, we were surprised to learn just how much they enjoyed the cookies as well so we took to the local farmers market. And here we are! With currently 5 stands around the world we have been able to share our unique, yet delectable salmon cookies!
